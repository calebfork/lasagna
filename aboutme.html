About Me

Caleb Forker

I was born in North-Central Missouri near Columbia, but have lived in Branson, Missouri since I was around five years old. Both my parents had fine art degrees and instilled a strong love for the arts within me as a young child. For as long as I can remember, I've been drawing, painting, and sculpting. My favorite media to use is pen and ink, but I find myself wanting more and more to start using digital media to create art.
